01 / About

Who Am I?

I am Ayush Kaute, an engineering student currently exploring Renewable Energy Management (REM) as part of my semester coursework.

My journey into the world of sustainable energy began with a simple question: "How can we power our future without compromising our planet?" This curiosity has led me to dive deep into solar technologies, wind power systems, energy storage solutions, and grid integration strategies.

Through this portfolio, I aim to document my learning, showcase my projects, and demonstrate my understanding of how renewable energy can transform our world. From theoretical concepts to practical applications, every assignment and case study has strengthened my commitment to contributing to a sustainable future.

02 / Curriculum

What I Study

Core topics in Renewable Energy Management

Solar Energy Systems

Photovoltaic technology, solar thermal systems, and grid-connected solar installations.

Wind Energy

Wind turbine technology, onshore and offshore wind farms, and aerodynamics.

Hydropower

Small and large-scale hydroelectric systems, pumped storage, and run-of-river plants.

Bioenergy

Biomass conversion, biogas production, and waste-to-energy technologies.

Energy Storage

Battery systems, pumped hydro storage, and emerging storage technologies.

Smart Grid & Integration

Grid modernization, demand response, and renewable energy integration strategies.
